About

This year has been a big year for 4th Grade and the use of technology in the classroom. The kids have been given numerous educational opportunities to supplement and enhance our current curriculum. Our newest addition has been Moby Max which is an awesome online resource that offers a captivating curriculum in all subject areas which is modified to meet the individual needs of each student. These lessons are aligned with the Common Core Standards and are presented in an engaging format for the kids.

Every night, Monday thru Thursday, there will be homework. If homework is completed each night that week, and done with care and effort, you will earn a no homework pass. This pass entitles you to not have any homework over the following weekend

We had some stellar scores for MAP Testing this past week, Awesome Work 4th Graders!!!
